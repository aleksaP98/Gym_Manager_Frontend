Who we are

Gym Manager is a platform for connecting all gyms in your region into a single entity. With our card subscription you can enjoy every gym for free. The card includes useage of all the gym equipmant plus a free Andorid or IOS app for monitoring your performance. The app has many funcionalaties including adding a goal for your training sessions, requesting help from a personal trainer in the gym and many more privileges.
